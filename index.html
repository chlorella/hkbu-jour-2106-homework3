Isn't Women choose to have gender pay gap?

It is well known that gender pay gap is exsit. According to the 2016's Global Gender Gap Report done by World Economic Forum, there is no country on earth where women make as much as men for the same work. In the same time, the report state that it would take another 170 years to close the global pay gap between men and women.

But someone still doubt in weather gender pay gap is exist, or it is only cause by women choose to be pay less.[ref]

As the result, Diversity Council Australia, released an important report together with KPMG and the Workplace Gender Equality Agency (WGEA). Called She’s Price(d)less – the economics of the gender pay gap, to dispaly the real picture underlying the gender pay gap.
